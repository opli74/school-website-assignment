Apply!

Midkent college has numerous courses ranging from catering to engineering.
You may be offered a course at both or only one of the campuses (Medway or Maidstone). The course titles here will take you to information about the qualification, including how long it will take to complete, course content, and fees. You'll also learn about your future opportunities once you've been qualified.

You can also check out the level and entry requirement of your desired course here.

You may also want to check out when the open days are here to get a better look at the campus and even chat to some of the students and teachers!

Courses
